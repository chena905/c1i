Obsession Origin
I originally thought Sonny Angels were super ugly, because why are the babies NAKED?? However, after getting gifted two animal series Sonny Angels on my birthday from my roommate, I completely changed my mind on these small trinkets. They are just so silly looking and funny. Afterwards, I went and bought the whole set of NYC Sonny Angels from Kinokuniya at Bryant Park. Money well spent, sorry dad.
