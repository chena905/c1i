Obsession Origin
It all started when I was five, wandering through the magical maze of Toys”R” Us. Luckily, I was there with my grandpa who always makes sure I get what I want. This started my collection journey with Calico Critters and I would name each little furry friend I got after my family and friends. The obsession never stopped and every time I walked past FAO Schwarz I would go in and get myself a Calico Critter blind box.
